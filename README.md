# AI-Screener-Survey-Analysis

AI Screener Dashboard – Recruitment Analytics
This project presents an interactive dashboard for an AI-powered recruitment screener launched by a recruitment firm. The AI screener is integrated with multiple job platforms and is designed to conduct pre–virtual interviews with candidates, helping recruiters save time and streamline the shortlisting process.

**Key Features**
**AI-Based Pre-Screening**

Candidates respond to automated screening questions before the virtual or in-person interview.

The AI captures and evaluates candidate responses in real time.

**Automated Shortlisting**

Based on candidate responses, the system classifies applicants as shortlisted or rejected.

Shortlisted candidates are advanced to the in-person interview round, reducing recruiter effort.

**Candidate Location Insights**

Visual representation of the geographical locations from where candidates have applied.

Helps recruitment teams understand regional talent distribution.

**Team & Brand Performance Metrics**
Displays the average number of candidates received per team or brand through the AI screener.

Useful for analyzing hiring demand and job posting performance.

**Job Platform Integration**

Tracks data across all job postings that use the AI screener.

Provides a consolidated view of screening outcomes across platforms.

**Impact**

Reduce manual screening time.

Improve candidate shortlisting accuracy.

Gain data-driven insights into hiring performance.

Optimize recruitment strategies using AI-powered analytics.
